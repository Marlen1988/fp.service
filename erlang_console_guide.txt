Erlang Console Access Guide

===========================

 **Installation**
----------------
sudo apt install erlang-base


**Connecting to Erlang Console**
-------------------------------
To connect to a remote Erlang session, use one of the following commands:

erl -name console@master.fp -remsh fp@master.fp
erl -name console@rt-server1.fp -remsh fp@rt-server1.fp
